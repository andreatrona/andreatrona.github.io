BOXE
Sound performance (2022)

BOXE is a sound performance centred on the use of objects and techniques from boxing as imagery and acoustic world. The study, which includes some characteristic elements of this sport in order to employ them as instruments within an electroacoustic musical approach, revolves around the idea of training, ring, fight and game, using all available objects and devices as dramaturgical elements. In BOXE the steps, blows, lights and live electronics become narrative possibilities within a study of sound, movement, agonism and absence.

Concept, live electronics, performer: Federico Pipia
Sound direction, live electronics, lighting direction: Andrea Trona

Developed during the residence at Sala Perriera - Cantieri Culturali Zisa in october 2022. First public performance: 31 october 2022, Prima Onda Fest 2022

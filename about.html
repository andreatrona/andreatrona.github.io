Andrea Trona is a sound designer, musician and programmer based in Bologna (IT). He works with sound in music, performance and multimedia fields, with a strong interest in non-conventional performative spaces, collaborating with artists and performers working as sound director, musician, developer and technician, performing works in Italy and abroad.

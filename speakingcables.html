Speaking cables
choreographic device for voice, cables and speakers (2023)

Speaking cables is a sound performance centred on the use of objects and techniques from boxing as imagery and acoustic world. The study, which includes some characteristic elements of this sport in order to employ them as instruments within an electroacoustic musical approach, revolves around the idea of training, ring, fight and game, using all available objects and devices as dramaturgical elements. In Speaking cables the steps, blows, lights and live electronics become narrative possibilities within a study of sound, movement, agonism and absence.

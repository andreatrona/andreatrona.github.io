site specific sound installation
Site-specific sound installation conceived for the entrance staircase of the Music Museum and its impressive acoustics, allows pre-existing musical fragments taken from Father Martini's musical repertoire to dialogue with sounds of an electro-acoustic nature and to make the spaces of Palazzo Sanguinetti resonate in unprecedented way.

Concept: Francesco Giomi
Sound design: Matteo Pastorello, Andrea Trona
Artistic coordination: Agnese Banti
